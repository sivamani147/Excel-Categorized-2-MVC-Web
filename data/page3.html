Type of Misconduct Absence due to Incarceration in Excess of Ten Days
Standards of Work: Employees absent due to incarceration shall be disciplined in accordance with the applicable collective bargaining agreement.
Reasonable Corrective Action(s) Discharge
Specific Factor(s) to be considered for Assessing Non-Progressive Corrective Action Less Severe:                                 Was the employee not given a reasonable opportunity to post bail? Less Severe:                              Was the employee subsequently released because the imprisonment was not justified?
